About GPTNotes

Leading-edge algorithms

GPTNotes combines cutting-edge speech recognition technology with intelligent algorithms to automatically transcribe audio into full PowerPoint presentations, detailed notes, and generates potential action items.

What's behind GPTNotes

Jerry Hu, the founder and developer of GPTNotes, is a passionate programmer with expertise in multiple programming languages, including Python, Swift, JavaScript, Node.js, HTML/CSS, and more. Born in Hangzhou, China, and currently residing in Vancouver, Canada, Jerry is a grade 10 IB student who embarked on a self-taught journey in various coding languages during the COVID-19 pandemic. Driven by a deep interest in computer engineering and artificial intelligence, Jerry has successfully completed numerous projects. Among his notable achievements is a bike tracker for his electric bike, offering similar functionality to an airtag but at a much more affordable cost. Additionally, he has developed a chat app named Postman. As the creator of GPTNotes, Jerry's vision is to provide a cutting-edge note-taking solution that harnesses the power of advanced language models, enabling users to efficiently organize and comprehend their thoughts with ease.

Key functionality

GPTNotes accepts a wide range of audio formats, including MP3, WAV, and M4A. GPTNotes offers an array of powerful features to revolutionize your note-taking experience. It leverages advanced speech recognition technology and intelligent algorithms to automatically transcribe audio into comprehensive PowerPoint presentations, detailed notes, and actionable items, saving you time and effort. With the integration of advanced language models, GPTNotes provides intelligent suggestions and insights, helping you capture and organize your thoughts more effectively
